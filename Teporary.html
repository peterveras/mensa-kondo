Storytelling Bootcamp

For over 130 years, the National Geographic Society has funded groundbreaking scientists and explorers and has helped them share their findings with the world. In an effort to provide their grantees with the knowledge, and skills, to continue sharing their work; the Storytelling Bootcamp initiative was created. These 'bootcamps' are workshops that teach the National Geographic explorers how to better communicate their work with a broad audience, and I had the pleasure of designing the look of all the material used in these workshops.

Credits

Dir, Creative Strategy: Jeff Honea,
Creative Director: Christos DeVaris,
Copy Writer: Sarah DeLucia,
Designer: Peter Verastegui
